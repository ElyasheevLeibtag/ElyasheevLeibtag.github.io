Midrasha

"Midrasha" the name of our group theory (and related topics) seminar at Weizmann. I am privileged to take active part the seminar. Here is a link to the Midrasha web site, including the recordings of many interesting talks.

Annual Midrasha "walkshop"

        A few years back, amidst the challenges of Covid-19 in 2020, I initiated a "mathematical field trip" for our research group. Over the course of three days, we explored both nature and mathematics through hiking and engaging discussions. This unique initiative has since become an annual tradition, where group theorists from different Israeli universities join us, fostering a vibrant and distinctive mathematical community experience.
